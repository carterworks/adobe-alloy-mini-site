About Alloy Doodads

Founded in 2020, Alloy Doodads began as a passion project to create unique metal artifacts that blur the line between functional design and art. Our craftspeople combine traditional metalworking techniques with modern design sensibilities.

Every doodad, widget, and thingamajig in our collection tells a story. Whether you're a collector, interior designer, or someone who appreciates fine craftsmanship, our pieces add character to any space.
